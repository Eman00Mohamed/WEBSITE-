Our Non-Technical Committees

"Fostering creativity, leadership, and community engagement through diverse non-technical initiatives and events

Human Resources

The HR Committee is responsible for creating a supportive and inclusive environment within the MSP Club by managing member recruitment, engagement, and well-being. We organize team-building activities, address member concerns, and ensure smooth onboarding processes. Our goal is to foster a positive club culture that encourages collaboration and active participation from all members.

Public Relations

The PR Committee is responsible for building and maintaining the public image of the MSP Club. We manage communications, promote club activities, and engage with both the internal community and external audiences through social media and events. Our goal is to enhance the club’s visibility, attract new members, and ensure our initiatives reach a wider audience.

Graphic Design

graphic design committee enhances the team’s visual identity through unique logos and designs, boosts engagement through creative visuals, supports promotional activities by influencing participation, improves user experience in digital platforms, and showcases MSP’s innovation and uniqueness in a visual engaging way

Video Editing

The Video Editing Committee crafts visually compelling video content that strengthens the team's brand identity, drives engagement through captivating storytelling, supports promotional activities with dynamic video campaigns, enhances the user experience on digital platforms with visually stunning videos, and showcases MSP's unique approach and innovative spirit.
